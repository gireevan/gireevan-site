What Does "NA Plot" Mean?

"Non-Agricultural" (NA) land status is the gold standard for real estate investment in Maharashtra. It means the district Collector has granted permission to convert the land use from agriculture to residential.

With a Gireevan NA plot, you get:

  • Individual 7/12 Extract

    Separate ownership document on your name.

  • Bank Loanable

    Approved by major banks for plot loans.

  • Immediate FSI

    Ready for construction with sanctioned FSI.
