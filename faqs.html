Common Questions

Everything you need to know about investing with us.

Is the land NA sanctioned?

Yes, all our plots are Collector Sanctioned Non-Agricultural (NA) plots with individual 7/12 extracts. You get a clear title immediately upon registration.

Can I get a bank loan?

Absolutely. Since our projects are NA sanctioned and RERA registered (where applicable), major nationalized and private banks provide plot loans up to 70% of the agreement value.

What is the distance from Mumbai/Pune?

We are located approximately 1.5 hours from Navi Mumbai and about 2 hours from Pune via the Expressway/Khopoli-Pali road.

Do you provide construction support?

Yes, we offer turnkey bungalow construction services. You can choose from our pre-designed sustainable villa models or work with our architects for a custom design.

Are site visits available on weekends?

Yes, we are open on all days. However, we recommend booking an appointment so we can arrange a dedicated sales executive and vehicle for your site tour.
